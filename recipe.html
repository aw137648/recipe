Stir Fry Recipe

Ingredients Directions
4 Chicken Breasts cut into 1 inch pieces Combine all the ingredients listed under marinade including the chicken and set aside for at least 15 minutes.
2 tablespoons light Soy Sauce Whisk together oyster sauce, soy sauce, vinegar, black pepper, half cup water and one tablespoon of cornstarch and set aside.
1 teaspoon minced Garlic Once the chicken has marinated, toss it in remaining two tablespoons cornstarch.
1 tablespoon Toasted Sesame Oil Heat two tablespoons oil in a large pan and add the chicken pieces. Don't overcrowd the pan and if your pan is not large enough, do this in batches.
1 teaspoon ground Black Pepper Cook the chicken pieces on high heat for 3 minutes on each side. Remove the pieces from the pan and set aside.
1/4 cup Oyster Sauce Add the remaining tablespoon of oil to the pan on high heat, and add the onions and bell peppers. Toss them in the pan for a minute or two till they brighten up in colour and have cooked slightly.
2 tablespoons Light Soy Sauce Toss in the ginger and garlic and cook for another minute.
2 tablespoon Vinegar Add the chicken pieces and sauce mixture to the pan.
1 teaspoon ground Black Pepper Toss everything well, and simmer for a minute or two till the sauce thickens and coats the chicken well.
1/2 cup water Serve hot with steamed rice or veggies on the side.

Lasagna Recipe

Ingredients Directions
1 lb. ground beef Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
1/2 lb. sweet Italian sausage Make the meat sauce. Add beef and sausage to a large skillet over medium-high heat, breaking it apart with a wooden spoon. Add the onion and garlic. Cook until meat is browned, about 6-8 minutes. Drain the fat from the meat. Return the meat to the skillet.
1 medium onion -chopped Add the tomato sauce, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, sugar, 2 teaspoons salt, 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning, 1 teaspoon basil, and the black pepper. Stir until well combined. Cover and reduce the heat to low. Simmer the sauce for 30 minutes. Stir in 2 tablespoons of the minced parsley.
2 garlic cloves -minced As the sauce cooks, make the ricotta mixture. In a large bowl, add 1 cup mozzarella, the ricotta, 1/4 cup parmesan, 2 tablespoons of the parsley, the egg, 1 teaspoon salt, 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning, and 1/2 teaspoon basil. Stir until well incorporated.
1 (15 oz.) can tomato sauce Cook the pasta al dente, according to it’s package directions.
1 (15 oz.) can crushed tomatoes
2 (6 oz.) cans tomato paste Assemble the lasagna. In a 13x9 inch baking dish, add a very thin layer of meat sauce (about 1/4 cup). Layer 3 lasagna noodles, 1/3 of the ricotta mixture, and 1 1/2 (level) cups of meat sauce. Repeat the layers twice.
1/2 cup water Add a final layer of the remaining noodles, meat sauce, 1 cup mozzarella, and 1/4 cup parmesan.
2 tablespoons sugar Cover with foil that has been sprayed with nonstick cooking spray. Bake for 45 minutes. Remove the foil. Bake 15 more minutes.
3 teaspoons salt -divided Let the lasagna cool for 15-20 minutes before cutting
